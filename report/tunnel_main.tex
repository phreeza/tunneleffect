\begin{abstract} 
\small{We repeat the 1960 experiment by Giaver, in which the tunneling current from a superconductor through an insulating film is measured to obtain the width of its gap. Our results and the discrepancies to the BCS Theory are discussed. We measure the lead energy gap to be $(1.4 \pm 0.1)$ meV.}
\vspace{1cm}
\end{abstract}
\end{@twocolumnfalse}
